> subagent workflow that translated the d100 5e master-guide
> (`injury_table_d100.csv`) into SHT's 4CS system and resolved its internal
> contradictions. Authoritative conventions come from **spec 23 (crit-injury)**
> and **spec 24 (status/treatment ladder)** — this table is the content layer on
> top of them. The owner said the original "is a starting point, I don't know if
> that's exactly how I want it," so treat this as a draft to react to and edit.
>
> **Translation applied:** cures → the spec-24 TreatmentTier ladder (None / Rest /
> FirstAid / FieldSurgery / HospitalCare / AdvancedMedical / PowerHealing /
> Regeneration / CloneReplacement); advantage/disadvantage & flat ±N → **Column
> Shift (CS)**; ability map CON→CON, STR→STR, DEX→AGL, INT→INT, **WIS→INS** and
> **Charisma→FAME** (owner-forks); rest-cycle → combat/strategic durations; every
> bleed/poison/burn/mental row carries an **origin gate**; single **monotonic
> severity axis** (low d100 = worse, high = better boon).

## Owner rulings needed (forks)

- Dominant-arm handedness (Lose an Arm, d100=3): assume right-dominant unless the character model flags left (spec 23 open Q2).
- Combat Amnesia (d100=11): design ruling to make Memory Loss near-permanent, curable only via PowerHealing/therapy (Science-gated), CloneReplacement, or Time-Walker rewind — honoring source cure 'None' + SHT permanence rule.
- Sprained Wrist (d100=43): SHT has no spell slots — ruling that a hand-somatic sprain is a flat −1CS on the power's activation roll rather than a chance to waste the power entirely.
- New Phobia (d100=48): ruling that a phobia is a persistent, re-triggering ROSTER condition (not a one-combat status), curable only at AdvancedMedical/psychiatric tier.
- Alcoholism (d100=64): ruling to model addiction as a personality Flaw (spec 03) feeding Drug Influenced episodes + relationship/morale system, rather than a flat stat penalty.
- Break Item / Gadget Mishap (d100=78): OWNER CALL — CUT the row, or remap the 5e-inventory 'random item breaks' to an SHT equipment mishap (damage/destroy a random carried gadget, or set off a carried grenade).
- Panic Gadget Trigger (d100=79): OWNER CALL — which SHT gadget/grenade fires and whether it is player-favorable; also a CUT candidate if random gadget-fire is too swingy.
- Convenient Fall (d100=80): whether loot-from-the-downed is a live SHT mechanic at all; if there is no body-looting system this row is flavour-only.
- Muscle Memory (d100=81): remap of 5e potion to an SHT stim/FirstAid consumable the ally spends — confirm which consumable and its Healthcare-gated quality tiers.
- Mercy-Kill Aura (d100=86): reframed as an AI finisher-intent bias (CI-11) rather than literal mind-control on all enemies; also flagged as a CUT candidate given it produces no wound record (injury=null).
- Charisma→FAME ability-set mapping applied to all social/persuasion bonuses on Impressive Scar (Body) d100=93 and Impressive Scar (Armour) d100=94 (and the FAME penalties on facial-scar rows) — owner to confirm the RULING ability-set map.
- WIS→INS ability-set mapping applied to every Perception/awareness, fear-resist, and Wisdom-save row (rows 2,8,9,38,39,48,61,66,67,69,70,71, etc.) — owner to confirm the RULING map.
- Adrenaline Rush (d100=100): 5e 'hit die' has no SHT equivalent — mapped to a single CON-scaled heal tick (Health = STA×2+STR, Bible 3.1); owner to confirm the exact heal amount.

## Contradictions resolved (from the 5e source)

- Mercy-Kill duplicates (source d100=10 area + d100=11 single-attacker) MERGED into one 'Mercy-Kill Aura' row (now d100=86). Both were the same INS-save finisher-intent beat with no wound record; kept the single-combat trigger and flagged as a CUT candidate.
- 'Pulled thigh' duplicate (source d100=49 Moderate/Days vs d100=65 Light/combat 1-minute) RESOLVED: kept the Moderate/Days injury as 'Pulled Thigh' (now d100=46) and kept ONE combat-move variant as 'Pulled Thigh (strain), −10ft' (now d100=72); the redundant Light −10ft duplicate's role is absorbed, with the lightest −5ft move penalty living as 'Limp' (d100=60). No two rows share the identical name+tier now.
- Broken Spine (source 15) vs Fractured Spine (source 23) — both emit Paralyzed. DIFFERENTIATED by a clean severity+cure ladder: Broken Spine = Critical / PowerHealing (d100=13); Fractured Spine = Severe / HospitalCare (d100=24). Kept both as distinct rungs rather than merging.
- Severed Muscles (17) vs Overstretched Muscle (31) and Severed Tendons (18) vs Overstretched Tendon (32) — identical −1CS STR/AGL effects. RESOLVED as legitimate Critical(AdvancedMedical)/Severe(FieldSurgery) permanent-vs-transient pairs, not true dupes; kept both with explicit tier separation.
- Ruptured Liver (27) vs Bruised Liver (41) — both Poisoned. RESOLVED as Poisoned II (Severe, d100=27) vs Poisoned I (Moderate, d100=40) severity pair.
- Dislocated Shoulder (72) vs Dislocated Finger (73) — identical −1CS attack. RESOLVED as a legit granularity pair kept apart only by cure tier (FieldSurgery/DC15 vs FirstAid/DC10), now d100=49 and d100=50; not merged.
- Severe Depression (51) vs Depression (58) — RESOLVED as roster-trauma (HospitalCare, Days, d100=61) vs combat-only self-resolving (None, until end of combat, d100=66) pair. Same for Uncontrollable Tremble combat (d100=58) vs trauma variant (d100=68).
- Non-ascending boon band FIXED: enforced a strictly monotonic axis where 1=worst injury and 100=best boon. The survivability/boon band now ascends by strength: Second Wind− (95) < Second Wind (96) < Second Wind+ (97) < Unexpected KO (98) < Unexpected KO+ (99) < Adrenaline Rush (100); the offensive/defensive one-shot boons (87–91) sit below the death-save survivability rows.
- All residual 5e spell levels ELIMINATED across every treatment cell — every row now uses the TreatmentTier ladder (None / FirstAid / FieldSurgery / HospitalCare / AdvancedMedical / PowerHealing / Prosthetic-or-Regeneration / Regeneration(power) / CloneReplacement).
- Every bleed/poison/burn/mental row now carries an explicit originGate (mandatory bleed gate on rows 12,14,15,16,26,37,47,51; mandatory poison gate on 27,40; mandatory MENTAL gate on 11,48,61-71); all penalties expressed in CS or a named SHT status.

## Summary

Reconciled the 100 translated SHT-native injury entries into a single monotonic table (d100 1 = most fatal, 100 = best boon), enforcing a strictly ascending severity/boon axis. Deduped every confirmed collision: merged the two Mercy-Kill rows into one, resolved the 'Pulled thigh' duplicate (one Moderate/Days injury + one combat-move variant, with Limp taking the lightest tier), differentiated Broken vs Fractured Spine by a clean Critical/Severe + PowerHealing/HospitalCare ladder, and kept the muscle/tendon/liver/dislocation pairs as legitimate permanent-vs-transient or severity rungs rather than true dupes. Purged all residual 5e spell levels — every treatment cell now sits on the TreatmentTier ladder (None → FirstAid → FieldSurgery → HospitalCare → AdvancedMedical → PowerHealing → Prosthetic/Regeneration → CloneReplacement). Every bleed/poison/burn/mental row carries a mandatory originGate, and every penalty is expressed in CS or a named SHT status (Bleeding I–III, Poisoned I–II, Paralyzed, Frightened, Berserk, Exhausted, Mute, Prone, Dying). Filled the owner-named thin categories from the crit spec — added permanent Lose-a-Finger, confirmed Ear/throat/tendon/rib coverage across two severities each. Flagged 13 owner-fork rulings (ability-set WIS→INS and Charisma→FAME maps, 5e-inventory rows 78–79 marked CUT-or-remap, hit-die heal on row 100, addiction-as-Flaw, phobia-as-roster-condition) that require the owner's design sign-off.
