================================================================================
TACTICAL COMPARISON
================================================================================

URBAN WAREHOUSE:
+ Best for: Close-quarters building assault
+ Cover density: Medium (pillars and external positions)
+ Sight lines: Long on exterior, restricted in building
+ Mobility: High outside, restricted inside
+ Choke points: 3 doors
+ Team separation: ~10 tiles

MILITARY COMPOUND:
+ Best for: Defensive holdouts and building-to-building combat
+ Cover density: High (buildings, walls, sandbags)
+ Sight lines: Restricted by perimeter wall and buildings
+ Mobility: Good in courtyard, restricted in buildings
+ Choke points: 3 gates, multiple doors
+ Team separation: ~20 tiles (across compound)

CITY STREETS:
+ Best for: Urban warfare, building clearing
+ Cover density: Medium (street barriers, building interiors)
+ Sight lines: Long on streets, blocked by buildings
+ Mobility: High on streets, restricted in buildings
+ Choke points: 4+ building doors, intersection
+ Team separation: ~14 tiles (across intersection)

================================================================================
SPAWN POSITION RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

With 30x30 maps, use these spawn positions for better tactical spacing:

BLUE TEAM (West):          RED TEAM (East):
  Alpha:      (2, 10)        Hostile1:   (27, 10)
  Sniper:     (2, 20)        Heavy:      (28, 15)
  Tech:       (1, 15)        Rocket:     (27, 20)
  Teleporter: (3, 12)        Pyro:       (26, 12)
  Gatekeeper: (1, 18)        Controller: (26, 18)
  Medic:      (1, 22)

This creates:
- ~25 tiles horizontal separation
- Vertical spread of 12 tiles per team
- Forces movement and positioning before engagement
- Allows snipers to find positions before combat starts
