# SuperHero Tactics - Vision & Design Questions

## 1. THE CORE LOOP - What Is This Game?

**1.1** In one sentence, what is the player trying to DO in this game? What's the ultimate goal?

**1.2** What does a typical play session look like? (30 min? 2 hours? What activities?)

**1.3** What games should I be thinking about for inspiration? You mentioned Jagged Alliance 2 - what else? XCOM? Crusader Kings? Something else entirely?

**1.4** Is this a campaign with an ending, or endless sandbox, or both modes?

**1.5** What makes the player feel like a badass? What's the fantasy you're selling?

---

## 2. THE WORLD MAP - Strategic Layer

**2.1** What does "controlling a sector" actually mean? What do you GET for controlling it?

**2.2** How do you TAKE a sector? Fight enemies there? Complete missions? Just show up?

**2.3** What's in sectors besides cities? Enemy bases? Resources? Random encounters?

**2.4** The militia system - explain it like I'm 5. Who are they? How do you train them? What do they do?

**2.5** Travel - should it cost money? Use fuel? Have random events during travel?

**2.6** What happens if enemies attack a sector you control while your squad is elsewhere?

---

## 3. COMBAT - The Tactical Layer

**3.1** When does combat START? You enter a sector and enemies are there? You get ambushed? You choose to assault a location?

**3.2** When does combat END? All enemies dead? You retreat? Time limit? Objective completed?

**3.3** What are the STAKES of combat? Can characters die permanently? Get captured? Lose equipment?

**3.4** What combat objectives exist besides "kill everyone"? Rescue hostages? Steal intel? Defend a point? Assassination?

**3.5** How does the AI vs AI combat mode fit in? Is that for autoresolving fights you don't want to play?

**3.6** Stealth/detection - can you sneak into places? Is there a "free move" phase before enemies notice you?

---

## 4. CHARACTERS & RECRUITMENT

**4.1** Where do you find new characters to recruit? Hiring screen? Find them in the world? Story missions?

**4.2** What makes characters different from each other? Just stats? Personalities? Special abilities? Backstories?

**4.3** Can characters LEAVE? Quit? Betray you? Die permanently?

**4.4** The merc personality system from JA2 - do characters like/hate each other? Affect morale?

**4.5** How many characters should the player have at once? 4? 12? 50?

**4.6** Are there "hero" characters that are special vs generic recruits?

---

## 5. SUPERPOWERS & ABILITIES

**5.1** How common are superpowers? Is everyone super, or is it rare?

**5.2** Can characters GAIN powers during the game? How?

**5.3** What's the range of power levels? Street-level (Daredevil) to cosmic (Superman)?

**5.4** How do powers interact with tactical combat? AOE attacks? Flight? Teleportation? Phasing through walls?

**5.5** Are there power TYPES that matter? (Fire vs Ice, Psychic vs Physical, etc.)

---

## 6. EQUIPMENT & ECONOMY

**6.1** Where does money come from? Missions? Controlling territory? Selling loot?

**6.2** Where do you BUY equipment? Store? Black market? Craft it?

**6.3** How much does equipment matter vs character stats/powers?

**6.4** Is there equipment rarity? Common, rare, legendary?

**6.5** Ammo and consumables - do you need to manage them?

**6.6** Vehicles - how do you get new ones? Buy? Steal? Find?

---

## 7. MISSIONS & OBJECTIVES

**7.1** Where do missions come from? Email? Phone calls? NPCs? News events?

**7.2** What TYPES of missions exist? Give me a list of examples.

**7.3** Do missions have time limits? "Rescue the hostage before midnight"?

**7.4** Can you FAIL missions? What happens?

**7.5** Are there main story missions vs side missions?

**7.6** How does the investigation system tie into missions?

---

## 8. FACTIONS & ENEMIES

**8.1** Who are the ENEMIES? Criminal organizations? Governments? Supervillains? Aliens?

**8.2** Do factions have territory on the map? Can you see who controls what?

**8.3** Can factions be allied with? Negotiated with? Or always hostile?

**8.4** Do enemies scale with player progress, or are some areas just harder?

**8.5** Are there named villain characters? Recurring enemies?

---

## 9. BASE BUILDING

**9.1** What IS the base? Underground bunker? Office building? Mobile HQ?

**9.2** What rooms/facilities can you build? Training room? Armory? Prison? Lab?

**9.3** Can your base be ATTACKED? What happens if it is?

**9.4** Is there ONE base or multiple around the world?

**9.5** How does base building tie into gameplay? Just bonuses? Or required for certain things?

## 10. TIME & PROGRESSION

**10.1** How long is a "campaign"? Days? Months? Years in-game?

**10.2** What happens as time passes? World events? Enemy gets stronger? New threats emerge?

**10.3** Is there a "doom clock" or pressure to act fast, or can you take your time?

**10.4** How do characters improve over time? XP and levels? Training? Just equipment?

**10.5** Day/night cycle - does it matter for gameplay? Stealth at night? Shops closed?
