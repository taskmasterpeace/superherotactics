Props on the Grid

Every item occupies whole grid cells and is sized exactly to them · cell = 44px (map-proto scale) · 2026-07-10
The rule (now enforced by the pipeline): a prop declares a footprint in cells (prop-footprints.json), normalize-props.mjs trims and scales its art to exactly cells × 44px (tall props keep extra canvas above, anchored at the feet), and grid-manifest.json tells the engine the footprint to snap, block, and render. No more 1.1-cell crates or 2.7-cell cars — placement is integer cells only.

Raw PixelLab props come at arbitrary canvases; normalized ones land on cells. The highlighted band at the bottom of each canvas is the blocking footprint; anything above it is visual height that overlaps the tile behind (drawn over, walked behind).
