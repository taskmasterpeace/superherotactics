SuperHero Tactics · Procedural Level System

Roll a compound. Any size. Every run different.

Seeded generator: roads, fenced perimeter with gates, multi-room buildings (offices, barracks, storage, labs) with doors that lock, props and loot placed on whole grid cells — then validated for connectivity before it's handed to you. Right-click anything for the word menu. Items have weight: anyone lifts a medkit; it takes SURGE to shoulder a barrel and only the RED SENTINEL walks off with a dumpster overhead — then THROW it (propane tanks explode). Every action speaks the same icon language: a badge over the actor's head. WASD/arrows pan the camera.

Grid law enforced: every prop, item, unit and wall occupies whole cells from grid-manifest footprints. Same engine rules as the slice: vision cones, LOS, structural damage, flight, doors.
