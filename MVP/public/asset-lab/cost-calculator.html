What does the art actually cost?

PixelLab bills in generations. Token (8-dir) = 3 gens. Template animations (walk, punch, throw, fireball, death — the 49 presets) = 1 gen/direction. Custom animations (gun-fire, energy beams, flight — no preset) cost ~1 gen per frame per direction (≈8×). Your plan = ~2,000 gens/month. The lever: lean on templates, keep customs to few directions/frames.
