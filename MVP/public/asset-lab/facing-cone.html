SuperHero Tactics · Facing System Prototype

2-way sprites + a vision cone.

The sprite only faces East or West (West is a free mirror of East). True facing — all 8 ways — is read from the cone and the gold pip on the ring, like Project Zomboid. Click a unit, click a tile to move (the cone swings to the real direction, the sprite mirrors L/R). Fire and the projectile flies the true angle. Notice the enemy's cone points away when it isn't looking at you — you still know its facing.

Select a unit.
cone = where they're looking (true 8-way)gold pip = facing on the ringsprite = only E/W (mirrored)

One generation per animation instead of 4–8. The tactical info (who faces whom, flanking) lives in the cone, not the sprite — so we get flanking readability *and* the cheapest possible art. This is the recommended system.
