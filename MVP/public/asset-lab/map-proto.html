SuperHero Tactics · Playable Slice

Walk the compound. Take the shot.

Click a hero, click a tile to move. Right-click anything for the JA2-style radial menu: doors can be Opened, Lockpicked, or Kicked (loud — the hostile turns toward the noise), crates Searched for loot, enemies Attacked, open ground gives Move / Face-this-way. Doors are real now: closed doors block movement and sight — the interior door is locked. Vision cones (wall-clipped) show what everyone sees; cross the red cone and he opens fire.

Everything here is ours: PixelLab tileset + props, our v3 characters + animations, game SFX. This is the seed of the vertical slice — same grid, walls and facing rules the combat engine will use.
