SuperHero Tactics · Map Prototype

Built from our own generated assets.

Every tile and prop here was generated by PixelLab — the grass/concrete ground came out of one auto-tiling tileset, the desk, server rack, cot, crate, cabinet, door, chair and plant are map objects — and the three fighters are our v3 characters (Surge, Red Sentinel, the soldier) on a real 44px grid with selection rings + a movement path. This is the JA2/RimWorld look, made from parts we control.
