SuperHero Tactics · Combat Slice · Location Pool

One engine. Many battlefields.

Pick a location below — a downtown street, an open field, a school, or the fortified compound. Same XCOM/JA2-but-advanced rules everywhere: click a hero then a tile to move, right-click anything for the radial (weapons on 1–4, F to fly, walls take structural damage and collapse into cover). Civilians wander the map and panic under gunfire — mind your fire, collateral has consequences. Every tile, prop and pedestrian here is generated from the game's own asset pipeline.

Data-driven: each location is a definition (terrain · props · spawns · civilian density · strategic context) the tactical engine reads — the same shape the world map will hand to combat. Missing art falls back to CSS/emoji and upgrades the moment the sprite lands.
