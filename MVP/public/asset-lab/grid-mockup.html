SuperHero Tactics · Flat Top-Down Grid — Mockup

This is what I'm picturing.

A flat top-down grid with our real sprites on it. Click a friendly unit (gold ring) to select, then click a highlighted tile to move — the sprite turns to face the way it walks (that's the 8 directions working). Hit Fire to send a straight-line projectile at the enemy. No isometric math, no depth bugs — movement and projectiles are dead simple, which is exactly why this is easier for us.

Select a unit (gold-ringed).
← tune the timing, then Fire. This is the "builder" knob.
