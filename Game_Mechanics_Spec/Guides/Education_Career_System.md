# Education & Career System

## Overview

Education and Career determine what your character **can do**. They affect:
- **Skills** you have
- **Research** you can perform
- **Investigations** you're good at
- **Equipment** you can access
- **Income** you earn

---

| 6 | Masters | 18 | 20+ | Rank 3-4, Lead Researcher |
| 7 | Doctorate | 22 | 30+ | Rank 4-5, Principal Investigator |
| 8 | Post-Doc | 24+ | 35+ | Rank 5, Cutting-edge research |

## Career Categories

There are **7 career categories**, each with unique strengths:

### 1. Medical & Life Sciences (MED)
**Focus:** Doctors, researchers, biologists

| Strength | Bonus |
|----------|-------|
| Investigation | Forensic, Biological, Medical |
| Research | Bio-tech, Medical, Mutagenics |
| Skills | Medicine, Forensics, Chemistry, Biology |

**Key Jobs:**
- Rank 1: EMT, Nurse, Physician, Pharmacist
- Rank 2: Surgeon, Radiologist, Forensic Pathologist
- Rank 3: Neurosurgeon, Pharmaceutical Researcher
- Rank 4: Virology Researcher, Biotech Researcher
- Rank 5: **Mutagenics Researcher** (LSW origin research)

---

### 2. Visual & Performance Arts (ART)
**Focus:** Artists, performers, media

| Strength | Bonus |
|----------|-------|
| Investigation | Social, Media, Cultural |
| Research | None (but great for undercover) |
| Skills | Performance, Deception, Social, Media |

**Key Jobs:**
- Rank 1: Model, Dancer, Musician
- Rank 2: Actor (+3CS Deception!), Stunt Performer
- Rank 3: Script Writer, Special Effects Artist
- Rank 4: **Film Director** (Media manipulation)

**Special:** Actors get **+3CS Deception** - perfect for undercover work.

---

| Strength | Bonus |
|----------|-------|
| Investigation | Political, Academic, Historical |
| Research | Historical |

**Key Jobs:**
- Rank 1: Librarian (+3CS Research!), Journalist (+4CS All investigations)
- Rank 2: Historian, Career Politician, Linguist
- Rank 3: Professor, Archaeologist

**Special:** Journalists get **+4CS to ALL investigations** - best generalist investigators.

### Research Team Composition

For complex research, you need a **team**:

```
Example: Building Power Armor Mk2

- Robotics Engineer (TECH Rank 4) - Lead researcher

Facility: Robotics Lab + Aerospace Lab access
Time: 30 days base (modified by team quality)
```

## Career Progression

### Advancing in Career

To advance to the next rank:
1. **Meet education requirement**
2. **Meet stat requirements**
3. **Spend time in current rank** (1-5 years depending on rank)
4. **Complete advancement task** (varies by career)

### Career Change

Characters can change careers:
- **Related career:** Keep 50% skill bonuses
- **Unrelated career:** Keep 25% skill bonuses
- **Start at Rank 1** of new career (unless education qualifies higher)

### Dual Careers

Some characters have **two careers**:

| Primary | Secondary | Bonus Effect |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| MED | TECH | +2CS Biotech research |
| TECH | PHY | +2CS Equipment optimization |
| PSY | PHY | +2CS Interrogation |
| BIZ | PSY | +2CS Negotiation |
| EDU | Any | +1CS Research in that field |
| ART | PSY | +2CS Deception |

