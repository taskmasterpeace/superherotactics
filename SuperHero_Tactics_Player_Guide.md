## Getting Started

### What is SuperHero Tactics?

You control a **government-backed organization** that recruits mercenaries and super-powered individuals to defend your country from superhuman threats. With **2472 days until alien invasion**, you must build a team, conduct investigations, engage in tactical combat, and navigate international politics.

### Core Game Loop

**Daily Strategic Phase:**
1. **Manage Resources** - Allocate funding and equipment
2. **Assign Team Activities** - Training, investigation, medical treatment
3. **Respond to Events** - Email alerts, world crises, faction politics
4. **Plan Operations** - Select missions and deploy teams

**Tactical Combat Phase:**
5. **Deploy Teams** - Position characters on tactical grid
6. **Turn-Based Combat** - Use powers, weapons, and environment tactically
7. **Handle Consequences** - Deal with legal, political, and medical aftermath

**Progression Phase:**
8. **Character Development** - Advance skills, careers, and technology
9. **World Impact** - Your actions affect global politics and relationships

## Combat System

### Initiative & Turn Order

**Initiative Formula**: `Initiative = AGL + (INS/2) + Threat_Level_Bonus + Power_Bonuses + Equipment ¬± Situational_Modifiers`

**Turn Sequence:**
1. **Calculate Initiative** for all combatants
2. **Highest initiative acts first**, then descending order
3. **Each character gets 3-6 Action Points (AP)** per turn based on powers
4. **Actions cost AP**: Move (1), Attack (2), Powers (3+), Items (2)

### Action Point System

**üì± Basic Actions (1-2 AP):**
- **Move**: 1 AP per square moved
- **Basic Attack**: 2 AP (weapon or unarmed)
- **Reload**: 2 AP (restore ammunition)
- **Use Item**: 2 AP (med kit, grenade, etc.)

**‚ö° Advanced Actions (3+ AP):**
- **Power Use**: 3-10 AP depending on power level
- **Complex Maneuvers**: 3-4 AP (grappling, disarming)
- **Environmental Interaction**: 3 AP (throw objects, break walls)

### Combat Resolution

**Accuracy Calculation**: `Hit Chance = Base_Skill + Power_CS + Equipment_CS + Environment - Target_Defense`

**Column Shift (CS) Results:**
- **White**: Complete miss or failure
- **Green**: Basic hit - normal damage
- **Yellow**: Major hit - +50% damage  
- **Red**: Critical hit - +100% damage + special effect

**Example Combat Turn:**
```
Captain America attacks with shield:
- Base Skill: +2CS (Shield Combat skill)
- Equipment: +1CS (Vibranium Shield)
- Leadership: +1CS (team coordination)
- Target Defense: Batman's dodge +2CS
- Total: +2CS attack bonus

Dice Roll: 78 (Yellow Result - Major Success)
Damage: Shield damage 25 + 50% major hit = 37 damage
Special: Shield ricochets back to Cap
```

### Critical Hits & Body Part Damage

**Critical Hit Effects (Red Results):**
Roll 1d6 to determine specific effect:

**Arm Damage:**
1. **ARM BLOW**: Weapon arm suffers -1CS until healed (6-8 weeks)
2. **DEAD ARM**: Weapon arm cannot be used until healed (surgery required)  
3. **ARM CHOP**: Permanent arm loss (-4 STR and DEX permanently)

**Leg Damage:**
1. **HAMSTRING**: -1d6 DEX + movement reduced by half
2. **LEG WOUND**: -1d3 STR + movement reduced  
3. **LEG CHOP**: Permanent leg loss (-4 STR/DEX + movement halved)

**Head Damage:**
1. **HEAD BLOW**: Stunned for 1 round
2. **CONCUSSION**: Stunned 1d4 rounds + INT damage
3. **HEAD TRAUMA**: Unconscious + permanent INT/INS damage

### Environmental Combat

### Object Throwing
**Any character can throw environmental objects as weapons:**

**Throwing Range Formula**: `Range = (Character_STR - Object_Weight/50) √ó Object_Range_Modifier`

**Common Throwable Objects:**
- **Trash Can**: 25 lbs, 8 damage, 1.0 range modifier - $50 replacement
- **Compact Car**: 2,800 lbs, 55 damage, 0.2 range modifier - $25,000 + Grand Theft Auto
- **City Bus**: 25,000 lbs, 120 damage, STR 60+ required - $300,000 + Federal charges

**Example**: Character with STR 70 throwing compact car:
- Range: (70 - 56) √ó 0.2 = 2.8 squares
- Damage: 55 base + STR bonus = 65 total damage
- Legal: $25,000 property + Grand Theft Auto charges

### Knockback & Wall Breaking

**Knockback Distance**: Equals attacker's Strength Rank (Major Success or higher required)

**FASERIP Strength Scale:**
- **Good (30-39)**: 4 squares knockback
- **Excellent (40-49)**: 5 squares knockback  
- **Incredible (60-69)**: 7 squares knockback
- **Amazing (70-79)**: 8 squares knockback

**Wall Breaking**: Target's Strength vs Material Strength
- **Wood**: STR 15+ breaks automatically
- **Brick**: STR 30+ breaks automatically  
- **Steel**: STR 60+ breaks automatically
- **Vibranium**: STR 90+ required

**Example Knockback Chain:**
```
Super Strength punch (STR 80 = Amazing rank):
1. Target knocked back 8 squares
2. Hits brick wall (Material Strength: Good 30)
3. Target's STR 50 vs Wall STR 30 = Goes through wall
4. Takes wall impact damage + continues 3 more squares
5. Legal: $15,000 structural damage + property destruction
```

### Flight & Altitude Combat

**7 Altitude Levels:**
- **Ground (0)**: Full interaction with environment and cover
- **Low (1-2)**: Some building cover, +1CS height advantage
- **Medium (3-4)**: +2CS height advantage, weather effects  
- **High (5-6)**: +3CS height advantage, severe weather penalties
- **Extreme (7+)**: Strategic movement, aircraft encounters

**Flight Actions:**
- **Takeoff**: Ground to Level 1 (2 AP, vulnerable during takeoff)
- **Climb**: +1 altitude level (1 AP, half movement)
- **Dive**: -1 level + extra movement (+1CS attack bonus)
- **Hover**: No movement but +1CS accuracy

**Building Limitations:**
- **Residential House**: 8-10 feet = Ground level only
- **Office Building**: 12-15 feet = Altitude 1 maximum  
- **Warehouse**: 20-40 feet = Altitude 1-2 possible
- **Stadium**: 100-200 feet = Altitude 5-6 possible

**Breaking Through Ceilings:**
- **House Ceiling**: STR 30+ required ($5,000 damage + family lawsuit)
- **Office Ceiling**: STR 35+ for drop ceiling, STR 60+ for structural
- **Military Facility**: STR 80+ required (federal charges)

### Status Effects & Medical Treatment

**Severity Levels:**
- **Level I**: Minor effects treatable with basic care
- **Level II**: Serious effects requiring hospital treatment
- **Level III**: Life-threatening requiring intensive care or cloning

**Common Status Effects:**
- **Bleeding I/II/III**: Ongoing damage, movement penalty, medical treatment required
- **Stunned**: Cannot act for 1-2 turns
- **Unconscious**: Cannot act until awakened or medical treatment
- **Poisoned I/II/III**: Stat penalties, ongoing damage, antidote required  
- **Burning**: Increasing fire damage, spreads to flammables

**Medical Treatment:**
- **Hospital Stay**: 1-90 days depending on injury severity
- **Medical Costs**: $5,000-$500,000 depending on treatment
- **Cloning**: Required for death or severe permanent injury ($1M+)
